Into Wanderlust - The Story

Never stop exploring

Travel is a passion...

London

My first destination was London, at the age of 20. I explored the city and tried many different foods, cultures and environments.

France

From Paris to Tolouse and Bordeaux. I explored multiple cities for the first time, stayed in a crappy hotel and had friends the let me crash at their place.

The Netherlands and Belgium

It's a shame I didn't know how to ride a bicycle back then, I could have explored much more. This time, not only did I visit different cities, but I also visited different countries.

Overseas - Japan

This is when I traveled outside of Europe for the first time. After a brief stop in Rome, where I enjoyed some delicious food, I flew over to Japan. I had the biggest culture clash in there.

Moving to Japan

In 2021, I finally moved to Japan, where I have been living ever since. Before moving here, me and my (now) wife decided to explore the rustic, and modern streets of Madrid, Barcelona and Toledo, in Spain.
